Monkeys

  • Cookie:

    • Cookie is a tree dwelling Red Colobus monkey that lives entirely on fruit leaves.

  • Earl:

    • Earl is an Allen's Swamp monkey. These monkeys are named after the American zoologist Joel Asaph Allen, who discovered this species in the swamp forests of Central Africa.

  • Banana Pudding:

    • Banana Pudding (Puddin' for short) is a colorful Douc Langur monkey that loves orangres and pears.
